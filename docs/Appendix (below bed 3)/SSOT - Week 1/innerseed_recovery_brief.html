02 — The Project

InnerSeed Garden is a mindfulness and guided session web application. Its purpose is to empower users to integrate positive change into daily life and find present moment awareness. The app has three session modes: Preset (ready-made sessions), Builder (custom sessions), and Creator (facilitator mode). Sessions draw on three content pillars: Ambient Music, Guided Breaths, and Guided Voices.

The key theoretical and commercial backbone is Michael Brown's The Presence Process, which informs both the sales philosophy and the app's approach to present moment awareness and conscious integration.

Core development philosophy: keep it simple — supergastric-case functionality. Design for the most natural, lowest-friction path for the everyday user.
