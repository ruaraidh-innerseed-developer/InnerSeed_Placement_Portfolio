The InnerSeed SSOT Workflow:
Step 1 — You take raw notes
During any session, focus call, or testing period, you write your own notes in Google Drive. Raw, honest, however they come. These are your hard copy. They never disappear.
Step 2 — You feed me the notes
You paste or upload them here. I read them, flesh them out, structure them properly, and build them into the SSOT in the right format — findings, badges, sections, screenshots, everything.
Step 3 — I display the full document
Before anything is saved, you see the complete designed document rendered here. You review it, request changes, approve it.
Step 4 — You download and file it
Saved as SSOT_Week1_10June.html or whatever the date is, into the correct week folder in Drive.
Step 5 — You test me
At the start of a new session, you upload the saved file and ask me to rebuild or continue from it. If I can do it accurately, the system works. If not, we identify the gap and fix the workflow.
