How this connects to the Flagship

Same engine, one layer more specific

Every mechanism this document assumes already exists in the Flagship and isn't repeated here: Claude's four passes (Insight, Strategy, Production, Quality), the human approval gate, Higgsfield's brief-and-generate handoff, Postiz's scheduling, Plausible's measurement, the end-to-end Backbone, and the five completion parameters that keep any input moving to a gate-ready state — the same parameters the simulation below tags as they fire. InstagramAwareness → Consideration

Purpose: the primary visual storytelling channel and the main organic workhorse for both cities. Where most people will first see the brand's actual look and feel, not just read about it.

Correction, not a from-scratch channel

@theinnerseeddevelopers already exists — 898 followers, 162 posts, active since November 2023, run by Jeroen and Chris around the in-person Enschede studio. The app has zero awareness on it today, but the account itself, the voice, and the audience are not starting from nothing. App content joins an existing account rather than launching a new one.

Content it carries

Reels (short vertical video — the reach engine), feed posts (stills, quote cards — the trust-building layer), Stories (behind-the-scenes, polls, lower production, higher frequency).

Funnel fit

Reels lead on Awareness — built for reach to people who've never heard of InnerSeed. Feed posts and Stories carry Consideration — saves, shares, and repeat views from people already circling.

Claude — copy→Higgsfield — video/image→Quality check→Human gate→Postiz→Instagram
Measured by

Reach and saves (Reels, native + Postiz), click-throughs to the site (Plausible, tagged by city and post), follower growth as a lagging trust indicator — never the headline number on its own.

Contextual example

UK · Edinburgh — a 15-second Reel of Ruaraidh's own taster-session footage, unpolished on purpose, captioned in his own voice, introducing him and the practice with no app mention. Awareness stage, full stop.

YouTubeAwareness → Consideration

Purpose: the deeper, slower-burning trust channel. Where Instagram earns a glance, YouTube earns minutes — the right place for the founder story, the "why this app exists" case, and content aimed at coaches sizing up whether to build on the platform.

Content it carries

Longer founder and practice pieces (Jeroen's own story, Ruaraidh's coach-training journey), "how it works" explainers, coach-facing walkthroughs of the B2B2C model.

Funnel fit

Awareness via search and recommendation for people actively looking ("breathwork app," "conscious connected breathing"); Consideration via watch time and depth — the format that turns curiosity into real trust.

Claude — script→Higgsfield — visuals→Quality check→Human gate→Postiz→YouTube
Confirmed, not assumed

Checked directly against Postiz's own documentation: YouTube is a native integration, not a workaround — title, description, tags, thumbnail, and public/unlisted/private visibility are all settable through the same scheduling flow as Instagram. No separate manual-upload step needed.

Measured by

Watch time and average view duration (YouTube Analytics — the real trust signal here, more than views), click-throughs to the site (Plausible).

Contextual example

NL · Enschede — a six-minute piece built from the Enschede founder footage, Jeroen speaking directly to the health crisis that led to InnerSeed, ending on the react-versus-respond distinction as the practical takeaway rather than a pitch.

XAwareness (B2B2C-leaning)

Purpose: the lightest-touch, lowest-production channel, and the one aimed less at Breathers and more at the wellness and coaching world — real-time conversation rather than polished output. New ground for InnerSeed; there's no existing presence or precedent to build from here. Owned by Jeroen — not a city-split channel like Instagram and YouTube. It runs in English, pitched at an international coaching and wellness audience, not as an Enschede-local extension of his Dutch-language work.

Content it carries

Short text posts and threads in Jeroen's own voice, commentary on breathwork and wellness discourse, direct callouts of specific app features, links out to the YouTube and website pieces.

Funnel fit

Almost entirely Awareness — visibility inside conversations the coach audience is already having, rather than a destination in itself.

Claude — copy only→Quality check→Jeroen approves→Postiz→X
Measured by

Impressions and replies as an early signal only — this channel earns patience before it earns a verdict, given there's no existing following to build from.

Contextual example

A short thread in Jeroen's own voice, responding to a wellness-industry conversation about burnout, tying it to the reacting-versus-responding distinction, ending with one line naming the app — commentary first, product mention last.

Paid adsConsideration → Convert
Pending budget — not yet active

No paid budget is allocated as of 16 July. This profile is written so the framework is ready the moment that changes — not as a channel to start producing for now.

Purpose: not a content-creation channel in its own right — an amplifier for content that's already proven itself organically. Given the zero-audience starting point, paid spend is what buys initial reach faster than organic alone can build it, without inventing a separate creative pipeline to do it.

Content it carries

The best-performing Instagram Reels and posts, re-cut as ad creative, paired with conversion-focused copy variants Claude generates specifically for the ad — not the original organic caption reused as-is.

Funnel fit

Mainly Consideration and Convert — retargeting people who've already visited the site or watched a video, plus tightly targeted cold reach (local radius, wellness interests) in each city.

Proven organic asset→Claude — ad copy variants→Quality check→Human gate→Ad platform directly
Two things stacked, not one

No budget is allocated, and even once there is, the five-instrument Stack has no ad-platform seat to spend it through — paid ads run through Meta Ads Manager or YouTube Ads directly, not Postiz, since scheduling tools and ad platforms aren't the same job. Whether a sixth instrument gets named here is a decision for once budget actually exists, not before.

Measured by, once live

Cost per click and cost per session-start (the ad platform's own reporting), then handed to Plausible for what happens after the click — the only measure this framework actually trusts.

Contextual example — for when budget exists

The Spotify-import Reel, once it's organically confirmed as a strong performer, re-run as a small-budget Instagram ad targeting a local radius around Edinburgh and Enschede plus wellness and meditation interests — proven creative, new audience, no new production cost. Not a plan for this week; a plan for whenever the first pound or euro is actually approved.

EmailConsideration → ConvertOwned channel · via MailerLite

Purpose: the one channel with no algorithm between the brand and its audience — owned, not rented. Where a sign-up becomes a relationship, and where the app's practice-support promise actually gets kept between sessions.

Content it carries

Welcome sequences per segment (Breathers vs. the coach routes, Dutch or English), practice-support emails, feature announcements (D-43 first), and re-engagement after a defined period of inactivity.

Funnel fit

Almost entirely Consideration and Convert — someone has to opt in before this channel exists for them. Its job is moving a sign-up toward a first session, then toward a habit.

Claude — copy per segment→Quality check→Human gate→MailerLite→Inbox
Measured by

Opens, clicks, and unsubscribes per segment (MailerLite); session-started goal completions from email referrers (Plausible) — the click matters less than what happens after it.

Contextual example

UK · Edinburgh — a first Edinburgh sign-up lands in the edinburgh_breather segment and receives an English welcome written in Ruaraidh's own introductory voice, ending with one specific, low-pressure step: a five-minute session to try tonight.

WebsiteAwareness → ConvertOwned channel · innerseed.garden

Purpose: the destination every other channel points at, and the only surface where Convert actually happens — a session started. Also the long-form home: the one place search intent can land on something substantial rather than a bio link.

Content it carries

Long-form articles in Dutch and English targeting Bewust Verbonden Ademwerk and Conscious Connected Breathwork intent, landing pages per route, and feature pages — D-43 first.

Funnel fit

Awareness via search, Convert as the destination — every click-through from Instagram, YouTube, X, email, or a future ad lands here. If this surface underperforms, every other channel's numbers lie.

Claude — long-form draft→Quality check→Human gate→Published to site→Plausible
Measured by

Pageviews and referrers by locale; the goal completion that counts is session started — the only measure this framework trusts, read per language and per referring channel.

Contextual example

Two same-theme articles live at once — Dutch for Enschede intent, English for Edinburgh — with Plausible reporting each apart, so "is the website working" always resolves to where, in which language, from which channel, never one blended verdict.

Claude — Insight & Strategy

The connection this piece makes

B2C Consideration. The March Reel earned trust by promising the practice continues at home — this piece is the evidence, the one concrete feature that makes "at home" different from a competitor's version of the same promise.

Claude — the brief Higgsfield would receive

The brief itself

Subject: a hand opening the app, choosing a personal playlist, watching it settle into a guided session. Reference asset: the real D-43 screen recording. Mood: the same evening tone as the March Reel — a continuation, not a new voice. Brand cues: light-mode palette, the app's own line — a moment for you — as on-screen text in the final second. Shot list: close on the phone screen, the playlist sliding in; cut to the session beginning; hold on the closing line.

Higgsfield — what it would return

A 15-second vertical clip, if generated

Would be built from the real screen recording rather than a text prompt alone — the import shown as the brief specifies, the transition from choosing music to beginning the session given room rather than cut quickly, ending on the line held long enough to read once.

Human gate

Jeroen and Ruaraidh approve

The same non-negotiable step as every other piece. Once there's an actual clip to look at, this is where it gets reviewed, same as everything else in the Stack.

Publish & measure

The account that already has 898 people on it

Not a cold launch — the same audience the March Reel already reached, shown the specific feature that post's promise was about.
